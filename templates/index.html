Explore Teyvat

Anemo

Mondstadt

The city of freedom that crowns the northeast of Teyvat. Under the protection of the Anemo Archon, Barbatos, the breeze here sings of history and freedom.

Geo

Liyue Harbor

A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat. The home of contract, trade, and stone monuments built by Rex Lapis, the Geo Archon.

Electro

Inazuma

An archipelago situated far in the east, surrounded by storms and lightning. The Electro Archon Raiden Shogun seeks "eternity" within these islands.

Dendro

Sumeru

The nation of wisdom and nature, where massive rainforests coexist with dry deserts. Governed by Lesser Lord Kusanali, the Dendro Archon.

Hydro

Fontaine

The nation of justice, technology, and art. Known for its gorgeous waterfalls and underwater diving. Guided by the principles of the Hydro Archon.

Features

Immersive Gameplay

Elemental Combat

Harness the power of Anemo, Pyro, Electro, Cryo, Hydro, Dendro, and Geo to trigger powerful elemental reactions like Vaporize, Melt, Overloaded, and Swirl to defeat monsters.

Vast Open World

Climb any mountain, swim across rivers, glide over the valleys, and explore spectacular ruins. Discover chests, solve puzzles, and uncover secrets across the lands.

Co-op Adventure

Team up with up to 3 other friends to clear domains, fight challenging bosses, complete quests, and explore Teyvat's landscapes together for richer rewards.
